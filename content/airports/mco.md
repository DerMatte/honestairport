# MCO Airport Guide

> Fast, no-fluff reference for clearing Orlando's four airsides and Terminal C with minimal walking and waiting.

## Quick Facts
- Four airside areas (1–4) served from landside Terminal C; Southwest is the largest carrier.
- Gate Link APMs connect airsides post-security within the west pair (1+3) or east pair (2+4) only.
- TSA PreCheck and CLEAR lanes at all security checkpoints.
- Crossing west↔east airside pairs requires exiting security and re-clearing TSA.
- Peak crowds 7-10am and 4-7pm; summer weekends worst.
- **MCT (cross-pair or separate tickets):** exit security + re-screen — budget **90 min** minimum.
- **Wi‑Fi:** Free **MCO WiFi** — browser login; sessions expire.
- **Power:** Outlets near Gate 100 (Airside 1) and central Airside 2 gates; charge before peak banks.
- **Water:** Refill fountains post-security in both terminals — bring an empty bottle.

## Security & Screening Tips
- Use the airport app to view live wait times before leaving the car.
- PreCheck and CLEAR are fastest at west-side checkpoints; east-side lines can move slower during peaks.
- Avoid 7-10am and 4-7pm windows; lines rarely exceed 25 minutes outside those blocks.
- Families and large groups should use the dedicated family lane at west-side security checkpoints.

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- Park in the Terminal C garage closest to your airside checkpoint to minimize landside walking.
- Grab food landside at Shake Shack (Terminal C) or Qdoba before security; airside choices are weak.
- Download the MCO app and enable push alerts for gate changes and security times.
- CLEAR members can skip the PreCheck line entirely at both terminals during peaks.
- If connecting across west/east airside pairs, exit security, walk landside through Terminal C, and re-clear rather than assuming an airside link exists.
- Rental car return is fast; follow signs to the new consolidated facility instead of older lots.
- Early morning flights (before 7am) often have empty security—arrive 60 minutes early.

## Terminals & Navigation
- Landside Terminal C is the main check-in building; airsides 1–4 are post-security only.
- Gate Link APMs connect airsides 1↔3 (west) and 2↔4 (east) without leaving security.
- Crossing west↔east pairs requires exiting security, walking landside, and re-clearing TSA.
- Common mistake: assuming any airside connects to any other — verify your pair before booking tight connections.

## Lounges, Food & Amenities
- The Club at MCO in Airside 4 is the only decent lounge; avoid if traveling basic economy.
- Best quick bites are landside: Shake Shack (Terminal C) and Qdoba.
- Quietest seating is near Gate 100 in Airside 1 after 8pm.
- No standout premium lounges for most travelers; plan to eat before security.

## Ground Transport & Parking
- Economy parking is cheapest but requires a shuttle; garage parking is worth the extra $8-10 for speed.
- Uber/Lyft pickup is on Level 2 of both terminals—fastest exit during non-peak hours.
- Avoid the old cell phone lot; use the new one near the rental car center for shorter waits.
- MCO has no direct rail link; Lynx bus 11 or 51 is the only cheap public option.
