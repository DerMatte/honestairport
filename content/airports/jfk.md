# JFK Airport Guide

> The most important things to know before you fly through New York’s busiest airport.

## Quick Facts
- **IATA**: JFK
- **Location**: Queens, New York City
- **Terminals**: 1 (international), 4 (Delta + international), 5 (JetBlue), 7, 8 (American + others)

## Security & Screening Tips
- JFK has multiple security checkpoints per terminal. Check your terminal and the airline app for the shortest lines.
- PreCheck and CLEAR are available in most terminals and usually worth it for frequent flyers.
- 3-1-1 liquids rule is strictly enforced. Use clear quart-sized bags.

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- **Terminal 4 Delta Sky Club**: Often less crowded than others; good for layovers.
- **AirTrain + Subway hack**: For Manhattan, sometimes faster/cheaper than taxi during rush hour (but watch luggage).
- **Charging + seating**: Terminal 5 has good power outlets near gates 20-30 and quieter seating areas upstairs.
- **Food tip**: Shake Shack in T4 is reliable; avoid the worst lines by ordering via app when possible.
